What is Deck Builder?

Deck Builder is a brand-new platform that uses the power of AI to generate flashcards for learning. All it needs is some text. Working with an analog source? Upload a photo and let the OCR do its work. Your flashcards will be quickly ready for usage.

Hover your mouse over the images for more info!

Upload an image or some text. You can edit it before letting the GPT-3.5-turbo model analyze it.
Our editor allows you to visualize your flaschards, edit or erase them. You'll be able to save them in your account as well.
Download your flaschards. They will be compatible with platforms such as Anki!
